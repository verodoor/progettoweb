the idea

I decided to create my football team website, since no one has done it yet. I thought this could be a really good opportunity to make more professional a completly auto-managed teams and at the same time develop very important skills. In general my team already have a stong presence on social media, and with this project my idea was tot further strengthen this presence on the web in general.

How the website is buildt

  • Title and carousel
  • Presentation of the team
  • Championship section
  • Presentation of our sponsors: Diadora and Ekostruct
  • Where we play, so fans can reach us easly
  • Contacts
  • the footer, with the logo and a rating form

The header

  • First there is the title, that change thanks to a A/B testing function.
  • The Header carousel, right after the title is directly implemented from Bootstrap 5 and has a small button above that redirects to all the sections of the page
  • Then the sling chant is there to give some animation effect.

Championship, where and partners

    I’ve put the direct link to the tournament where we play, so fans can stay always updated. I’ve done the same thing for the where with a google maps map.

Regarding the sponsorhip , I’ve inserted another carousel with some images of previous shootings done with them. Furthermore I’ve inserted two link swith the referral pages of this campaingns.

Contacts

In the contact information section I’ve put two icons that are directly linked to the Instagram account and my Whatsapp number. Furthermore there is a form where insert your email. These three way to contact us are directed both to fans and sponsor, particularly the email form to send everytime updated on matches and create a community.

The footer

  • At the bottom of the page there is a footer with the logo of the team
  • I also put a rating system to collect the users’ opinion about the webpage. It is very simple, with 5 star icons that are clickable.

The tools
