Who we are

Welcome to our website! We are a female football team in the heart of Milan, founded in 2020.In our vision nect with people and to make more and more people discover the beauty of women's football. Other than playing ,we do much more: by proudly setting foot in a tradition that has always kept football a male sport, the Maradonne manage to lightly appropriate, both on and off the field, this context considered by many to be "sacred". Our goal is to bring this sacredness back into an everyday and inclusive dimension.

Championship

For this season, we play in the CSI championship (7-a-side). If you want to follow and support us take a look at the calendar!

Partnerships

There are already some realities that support us in our mission every day: we are talking about Ekostruct and Diadora (take a look to our interwiew!)
